# Social Proof Integration Spec

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Customer Testimonials Display
The system SHALL display authentic customer testimonials that build trust and demonstrate service quality.

#### Scenario: When potential customers are evaluating Striker Mechanical, they should see evidence of past customer satisfaction to build trust in the service quality.
  - **Given**: A visitor views the landing page beyond Phase 1 content
  - **When**: They reach the testimonials section
  - **Then**: They see authentic customer testimonials with names and identifiers
  - **And**: Testimonials address specific service outcomes (reliability, expertise, honesty)
  - **And**: Visual indicators of satisfaction (ratings, checkmarks) are displayed
  - **And**: Testimonials are presented in a clean, credible format

### Requirement: Before/After Work Showcase
The system SHALL showcase before/after work examples that demonstrate mechanical expertise and quality workmanship.

#### Scenario: When visitors want proof of mechanical expertise and quality workmanship, they should see visual evidence of successful repairs and improvements.
  - **Given**: A visitor scrolls to the work showcase section
  - **When**: They view the project examples
  - **Then**: They see before/after images of actual mechanical repairs
  - **And**: Each project includes a brief description of the problem and solution
  - **And**: Images are presented in a professional, organized layout
  - **And**: Showcase demonstrates the breadth of mechanical capabilities

### Requirement: Business Credentials Display
The system SHALL display business credentials and qualifications that reassure visitors about professionalism and expertise.

#### Scenario: When visitors need reassurance about professionalism and expertise, they should see evidence of qualifications and experience.
  - **Given**: A visitor evaluating business credibility
  - **When**: They look for credentials and certifications
  - **Then**: They see years of business experience prominently displayed
  - **And**: Any relevant certifications or licenses are shown
  - **And**: Professional affiliations or memberships are indicated
  - **And**: Trust badges or seals of approval are displayed where applicable

### Requirement: Service Area Coverage
The system SHALL provide clear information about service area coverage to help potential customers determine availability.

#### Scenario: When potential customers want to know if Striker Mechanical serves their location, they should find clear information about service coverage.
  - **Given**: A visitor in the target market area
  - **When**: They look for service area information
  - **Then**: They see geographical coverage details clearly stated
  - **And**: Any travel radius or service limitations are explained
  - **And**: Special service areas or mobile service options are mentioned
  - **And**: Contact methods for location questions are provided

## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Enhanced Contact Information
The system SHALL enhance contact information with social proof context to increase visitor confidence and conversion likelihood.

#### Scenario: When visitors decide to contact Striker Mechanical after seeing social proof, they should have additional context and confidence in their contact decision.
  - **Given**: A visitor has viewed testimonials and work examples
  - **When**: They proceed to contact information
  - **Then**: Contact section includes trust indicators from social proof
  - **And**: Emergency or priority contact options are highlighted
  - **And**: Expected response times or availability information is provided
  - **And**: Contact section references the credibility established in previous sections
