# Content Expansion Spec

## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Detailed Service Information
The system SHALL provide comprehensive service information that addresses visitor questions and concerns in depth.

#### Scenario: When visitors want to understand specific services in depth, they should access comprehensive information that addresses their questions and concerns.
  - **Given**: A visitor is interested in a specific service
  - **When**: They click on or expand a service offering
  - **Then**: They see detailed descriptions of the service process
  - **And**: Common problems addressed by the service are explained
  - **And**: Expected timelines and outcomes are provided
  - **And**: Pricing information or ranges are available
  - **And**: Technical details are explained in accessible language

### Requirement: FAQ Section Implementation
The system SHALL provide a comprehensive FAQ section that answers common questions without requiring visitor contact.

#### Scenario: When visitors have common questions about services, pricing, or processes, they should find quick answers without needing to contact the business.
  - **Given**: A visitor has questions about Striker Mechanical services
  - **When**: They access the FAQ section
  - **Then**: They find answers to common questions about:
    - Pricing transparency and payment methods
    - Warranty information on repairs
    - Service timeline expectations
    - Emergency service availability and process
    - Diagnostic procedures and costs
    - Parts sourcing and quality
  - **And**: FAQ items are organized by category for easy scanning
  - **And**: Expandable/collapsible functionality for easy navigation

### Requirement: Pricing Structure Transparency
The system SHALL provide clear, upfront pricing information that builds trust and eliminates cost uncertainty.

#### Scenario: When visitors want to understand costs, they should find clear, upfront pricing information that builds trust and eliminates uncertainty.
  - **Given**: A visitor considering Striker Mechanical services
  - **When**: They look for pricing information
  - **Then**: They see diagnostic fee structure clearly explained
  - **And**: Hourly rates for different types of work are displayed
  - **And**: Any service package options or deals are presented
  - **And**: Additional costs (parts, materials) are explained
  - **And**: Payment methods and terms are specified

### Requirement: Emergency Services Information
The system SHALL provide clear emergency service information that helps visitors quickly understand urgent care options.

#### Scenario: When visitors have urgent mechanical repair needs, they should quickly find information about emergency service options and availability.
  - **Given**: A visitor with an urgent mechanical problem
  - **When**: They look for emergency service information
  - **Then**: They see emergency service availability clearly stated
  - **And**: Priority contact methods are prominently displayed
  - **And**: Emergency service hours and response times are specified
  - **And**: Any additional costs for emergency service are explained
  - **And**: Types of issues considered emergencies are defined

## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Enhanced Service Presentation
The system SHALL present services with enhanced detail and actionable information after Phase 2 implementation.

#### Scenario: When visitors explore services after Phase 2 implementation, they should find more detailed and actionable service information.
  - **Given**: A visitor has viewed social proof and basic services
  - **When**: They explore service offerings in detail
  - **Then**: Service descriptions link to detailed information
  - **And**: Service presentation includes pricing indicators
  - **And**: Emergency service options are highlighted where relevant
  - **And**: Service information addresses common customer concerns

### Requirement: Expanded Contact Options
The system SHALL provide tailored contact options that align with visitors' expanded knowledge and specific needs.

#### Scenario: When visitors decide to contact Striker Mechanical with expanded knowledge, they should have contact methods tailored to their specific needs.
  - **Given**: A visitor has detailed service and pricing information
  - **When**: They choose to make contact
  - **Then**: Contact options include emergency vs. non-emergency channels
  - **And**: Contact forms include service-type selection
  - **And**: Expected response times are specified for each contact method
  - **And**: Contact references the detailed information they've reviewed
